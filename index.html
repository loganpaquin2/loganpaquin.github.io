About

I don't just find spaces. I see what they're about to become.

Eight years deep in commercial real estate — from restaurant asset sales and tenant rep in Nashville to leading national site selection for one of the fastest-growing wellness franchises in the country. Where It Started

Paquin Farms & French Fort Vineyards

The Paquin name in business goes back nearly four decades. My father, Jeffrey D. Paquin, founded Paquin LLC in 1986 — a firm built on collaborative innovation, creative solutions, and bringing organizations and people together. That same philosophy shaped Paquin Farms, our family's property on Paquin Road in Kentucky, which Paquin LLC converted into a hospitality destination with cabins, Airbnb stays, weddings, and events.

As part of Paquin Farms, we built French Fort Vineyards — Edmonson County's largest vineyard — where I served as President. We planted over an acre of Chambourcin grape vines with help from Western Kentucky University's Viticulture Department and earned Kentucky Proud certification. The vineyard, the farm, and the vision behind them taught me that real estate isn't just about the building — it's about what happens inside it.

That lesson became the foundation for everything I do today. Now, through Paquin Capital, I'm carrying that vision forward with Paquin Park — the next generation of Paquin hospitality.

Point of View

On the future of retail

The best-performing retail isn't selling products — it's selling belonging. The brands winning today are the ones building third places that pull people off their screens and into rooms together. That's the thesis I bet on every day.

I find these deals the same way I always have — walking submarkets, reading rooms, building relationships that don't fit in a database. But I move faster now because I pair that instinct with AI-driven analytics, market modeling, and data tools that let one person operate like a team of ten.
